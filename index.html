About

Swetha Baskaran (she/her)

I am a learning experience designer and project manager based in the United States, where visual communication is totally my cup of tea. The brewing process began at the age of 10, thanks to countless Pictionary game nights with my friends. My initial dream was to become a Disney film writer, desiring to create magical moments on the big screen!

Of course, my plan did not work out, and I found my true calling through plan B. I discovered a whole new world to create magical moments that facilitate learning and empowerment through the captivating realm of experience design. From that moment forward, there's been no turning back.

Today, I find myself crafting my dream lifestyle, doing what I'm incredibly passionate about. It's a journey filled with creativity, inspiration, and the satisfaction of making a positive impact through my work. In my spare time, you can find me binge-watching some of my favorite sitcoms, rock climbing, or simply thinking of my next tea time!

Services

Bespoke Learning Experience

Visual design, eLearning development, UI/UX design, interactive guides, game development, video animations, innovative learning apps, augmented and virtual reality experiences, explainer videos, podcast creation, and data analysis – all combined to craft a customized solution tailored to your unique needs.

Animated Gaming Simulations

Ready to add a dash of fun to your learning journey? Let's explore where learning and gaming collide! Who doesn't adore a good game? My approach blends educational theory with captivating storytelling, creating an immersive gaming adventure. Picture beloved childhood games like UNO, Tic Tac Toe, and crosswords – they're not just nostalgic but also fantastic tools to reinforce new knowledge in an exciting way!

Project Management

Being an instructional designer means wearing many hats – from needs analysis to creative brainstorming, scripting, designing, rigorous testing, and launch, all with a touch of project management magic behind the scenes. I take the reins of the design process while collaborating with stakeholders to create the most impactful learning experiences. Whether it's crafting project schedules or diving into data analysis, I'm your one-stop-shop for all things instructional design and project management.
Let's turn learning into an adventure!
