About

Swetha Baskaran (she/her)

I am a learning experience designer and project manager based in the United States, where visual communication is totally my cup of tea. The brewing process began at the age of 10, thanks to countless Pictionary game nights with my friends. My initial dream was to become a Disney film writer, desiring to create magical moments on the big screen!

Of course, my plan did not work out, and I found my true calling through plan B. I discovered a whole new world to create magical moments that facilitate learning and empowerment through the captivating realm of experience design. From that moment forward, there's been no turning back.

Today, I find myself crafting my dream lifestyle, doing what I'm incredibly passionate about. It's a journey filled with creativity, inspiration, and the satisfaction of making a positive impact through my work. In my spare time, you can find me binge-watching some of my favorite sitcoms, rock climbing, or simply thinking of my next tea time!

I take the reins of the design process while collaborating with stakeholders to create the most impactful learning experiences. Whether it's crafting project schedules or diving into data analysis, I'm your one-stop-shop for all things instructional design and project management. Let's turn learning into an adventure!

LocalThrift App

Thrift Smart, Shop Local, Save Big!

Challenge: limited clothing styles and shopping options in Amherst

  • Limited access for shopping around Amherst, MA.
  • Need affordable clothes, new styles & access to unique clothing.
  • Students hoarding clothes & unable to de-clutter closets.
  • Promote sustainable clothing.

Solution: Thrifting Platform

  • LocalThrift is an online platform where users can buy and sell clothes at their convenience.
  • Provides an accessible & affordable shopping experience.
  • Sellers and buyers can meet for trade in person at local location.
  • Helps the sellers to de-clutter and promote sustainability.

Research Questions:

  • How easy is it for users to navigate the app and complete tasks such as browsing for items, selling an item?
  • What usability issues do users encounter while using LocalThrift?

User Evaluation: Participants complete a list of tasks on LocalThrift

  • Sell an item.
  • Browse for item.
  • Add items to cart.
  • Contact Seller.
